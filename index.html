NeuralIR is applying AI to scientific information retrieval (IR).

New methods are needed to deal with the exponential growth in research literature, as well as new types of research products such as tables, graphics, taxonomies, software code and datasets.

Bringing these scattered information types together in the cloud, just one-click away, allows more productive scientific workflows.

NeuralIR is also committed to making AI more democratic.
